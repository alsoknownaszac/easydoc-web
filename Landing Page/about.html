About The CEO
DR. ODIKPO IKECHUKWU BMBCH, DTM, FIHMN, President / CEO

Dr Odikpo Ikechukwu is a trained Medical Doctor, an experienced Business Man, and Philanthropist. Over the years he trained and worked in various medical Institutions locally and internationally acquiring diverse experience for 35 years in Family, Medicine, Travel Medicine and Aesthetic Medicine, Obstetrics and Gynecology.

Currently he is undertaking the Community Health Workers Program under edx Harvard University Dr Odikpo Ikechukwu is a Paul Harris Fellow, an alumnus of The Swiss Tropical Institute, Basel Switzerland and a member of the Ikoyi Club 1938. Hobby’ enjoys Football, Golf and Conflict resolution.

About the Vice President /Executive Director Management
MRS ENE CECILIA ODIKPO

Mrs Ene Cecilia Odikpo is a trained business manager / administrator graduated from University of Jos in 1989. Ene Odikpo is an alumnus of ESUT business school, a member of the Ikoyi Club 1938, a devoted christain. She is married with children and hobby is reading and watching christain movies.
